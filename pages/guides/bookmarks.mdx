# Bookmarks
Having quick access to the sites and references that are helpful and important to you is a must. With the bookmarks feature, you can add any site you want to your bookmarks list and access it with a single click.

Putting bookmarks into bookmark folders is also extremely helpful and important, especially if you have a lot. Organization is always important to an efficient workflow.
